About us

Our company “ZopShop” started own history in 2015 when our director first time visited China and impressed by it colorfulness and a wide range of differents decorster staff. We opened our first shop in 2014 in Almaty, then another one in Astana. After 3 years we decided to focus only on one product and fully concentrate on it by giving to it all our ideas and enthusiasm. Therefore, our company visited one of the factors, which produce vases and create an agreement between us. SO, during one a half year we get the most wonderful vases in our country and sell it around all the world. Our mission is to give an ability to get a part of different cultures by selling different vases. We have principles which help us to keep our clients satisfied by our service, firstly, the opinion of client should be on the first place, if accidentally we don’t have the example which will be appropriate we can make it by order, but it will take more time. Secondly, there are no borders to your fantasy. we are open for crazy ideas and always ready for complicated orders. We promise after our vases you will never change your choice.
